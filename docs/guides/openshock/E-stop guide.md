# E-Stops 101

::: info E-Stops for what?
E-Stops are a Physical button or switch that can be triggered to stop all curent and pending shocks, viberations, And beeps.  

:::
## What you need
- [OpenShock account](https://openshock.app/)
- [A connected shocker](./first-setup.md)
- [A Hub With A E-Stop Button](/diy/E-Stop.md)

## How to Use the E-stop

1. How to activate the E-Stop
   Press the E-stop button for Half a second,
   All shocks, vibrations and beeps will be halted, and all the hub will recject all Commands until the E-stop is reset.
    :::
   **That's it.**
2. How to reset the E-Stop
   Press and hold the E-Stop button for 10 seconds, this will release the E-Stop and all new commands to be send to the shockers
    :::

::: tip
The E-Stop is a safety system to protect you and those arround you, if your having an emergency or can not handle the shocks pressing it is a safe way to give your self sometime to recover
